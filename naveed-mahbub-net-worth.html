Naveed Mahbub Full Overview and Wiki

The Multifaceted Journey

His career trajectory is as colorful as a Bangladeshi rickshaw art piece—vibrant, unexpected, and deeply engaging.

From the technical rigors of electrical engineering to the spotlight of stand-up comedy, Mahbub’s journey is a testament to versatility and passion.

Engineering and Corporate Achievements

His early career was steeped in engineering, marked by significant roles at Ford Motor Company, Kyocera Wireless, and Qualcomm in the United States.

His leadership prowess shone through as he ascended to CEO positions at IBM and Nokia Siemens Networks Bangladesh.

These stints not only fortified his financial foundation but also honed a diverse skill set beyond the realms of technology.

The Comedy Arena

Transitioning from corporate boardrooms to comedy clubs, Mahbub founded Bangladesh’s first comedy club in 2010, a groundbreaking move that catalyzed the local stand-up scene.

His performances in the United States during the 2000s laid the groundwork for his comedic path, which later included a feature on Comedy Central and a best male comedian award at the Las Vegas Comedy Festival in 2007.
