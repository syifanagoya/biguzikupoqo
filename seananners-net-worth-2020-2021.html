Early Life

He was born on June 12, 1984, in San Diego, California. His mother is Mat Montoya. He attended San Diego State University, where he graduated with a degree in “television, film, and new media production.” He made many short films during his time at the University.

Initially, he had great difficulty in securing a stable job. So, he started off with freelance wedding filming. At the time, he was inspired by other gaming commentaries on YouTube, and thus, he decided to upload his own commentary as well. This marked the start of his successful career as a Youtuber, which eventually led to other opportunities.

He is also dating Cathy Diep, a woman he met while working at Machinima Inc.

Career

He initially started out by making CoD: WaW videos. However, he eventually moved to just CoD games and then just games in general. After his initial uploads on YouTube gained positive reviews from viewers, Adam uploaded a video on December 3, 2009, that he would henceforth be collaborating with Machinima Inc.

He gained increased popularity as a result of “collabs” with GassyMexican, Mr. Sark, Uncle Slam, Terroriser, Mangaminx/TheRPGMinx, and Chilled Chaos, RitzPlays, BryceGames, and Tejbz.

In November 2014, Montoya announced that he, along with his colleagues, had launched a new network, MCN, JETPAK. The reason for such a measure was to create an environment where YouTubers and Creators were not taken advantage of. The MCN is run and maintained by former employees of Machinima Inc.

The year 2016 was an exciting one for Montoya, for he announced that he, along with Scott Robison and VanossGaming, would be starring in a YouTube Red original series, The Paranormal Action Squad. It is basically an animated comedy aimed at mature audiences.
