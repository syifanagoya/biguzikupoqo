Meet Hazel Krasinski - Emily Blunt &amp; John Krasinski's Daughter

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, February 19, 2024

Hazel Krasinski is the daughter of Emily Blunt and John Krasinski. Born on February 14th, 2014, the American celebrity kid is 9 years old.

Hazel is just 9, but the youngster already displays some entertainment skills. According to her dad, the young lady loves to crack jokes. Hazel has enrolled in school, but her academic institution’s name is unknown. Emily just said her daughter is in a proper school with a uniform, so she is not going through the homeschooling process.

How Old is Hazel Krasinski?

Hazel Krasinski’s current age is pegged at 9 as the American celebrity kid was born on February 14th, 2014. Her place of birth is captured as the United States, precisely at the Medical Center of St. Elizabeth, located in Boston, Massachusetts. This obviously makes the youngster an American by nationality. Talking about Hazel’s ethnic background, the Boston native is of White ancestry. Her mother is of English roots, while her dad comes from a mix of Polish and Irish.

Hazel’s birth was announced by her dad via Twitter in a message that said how incredibly happy he and his wife were to welcome their first fruit into the world.

Wanted to let the news out directly. Emily and I are so incredibly happy to welcome our daughter Hazel into the world today! Happy bday!

Earlier in September 2013, Hazel’s parents announced her impending arrival, and in December, they held a lavish baby shower.

Hazel was almost three before her dad shared her first picture on Twitter. According to the proud father, children’s pictures ought to come directly from their parents, and in the caption, he expressed how thrilled he was to introduce Hazel to the public.

Pics of kids should only come direct from parents. So I'm thrilled to introduce you guys to Hazel!!! #NoKidsPolicy pic.twitter.com/qVvUTRdllP

Nothing much is known about Hazel Krasinski’s academic journey, but her mum told fans on the set of a 2020 edition of Jimmy Kimmel Live! that her children are going to what she described as a “proper school with proper uniforms”. The actress also said they are “sounding quite British.”

Who is Hazel Krasinski’s Father?

The youngster’s dad has been identified as John Burke Krasinski. Born on the 20th of October 1979, John is the last child of Mary Clare (née Doyle) and Ronald Krasinski. He grew up alongside two brothers and was raised a Catholic.

John is an alum of Brown University and also attended National Theater Institute to study theatre arts. Today, he is as an actor cum movie maker with several productions to his credit. He is best known for the situational comedy, The Office on NBC, where he breathed life into the character of Jim Halpert. Hazel Krasinski’s dad functioned as the series producer cum director. John has also appeared in other movies and TV shows.

Since he became a dad, the actor and movie maker has been ecstatic, and he prides himself as a hands-on father. Talking to PEOPLE in 2016, John said fatherhood altered his life to a great deal; he started experiencing some existential crisis right from the moment Hazel announced her entrance. Krasinski said it was really serious, like having a mirror held up to you with someone watching your every move, and you know that you must lead by example. Becoming a father made John give up some things and concentrate on becoming a better person for the sake of his children.

Hazel’s Mum is Also an Actress

Just like her dad, Hazel Krasinski’s mum is also part of the entertainment industry as a British actress. Born in London, on February 23rd, 1983, Emily Olivia Laura Blunt is the daughter of Former actress cum teacher, Joanna Blunt, and Oliver Blunt, who works and earns a living as a legal practitioner. Emily grew up with three siblings – Felicity, Sebastian, and Susannah and she is the second child.

She passed through Ibstock Place School located in Roehampton, southwest London. Later, she attended a private sixth-form college for performing arts called Hurtwood House.

Emily made her acting debut on stage and later moved into onscreen roles. The British actress achieved her breakthrough in 2006, starring in the TV film, Gideon’s Daughter and the comic-drama movie entitled The Devil Wears Prada.

Hazel Krasinski has One Sibling Named Violet

Hazel is already a big sister to Violet Krasinski, who announced her entrance in 2016.

Hazel was barely one year when John and Emily announced on Entertainment Tonight that they were ready to give her a sibling. John has been back on Entertainment Tonight after the birth of Violet Krasinski, and according to the actor, Hazel has been such an amazing big sister.

Just in the same way, he announced Hazel Krasinski’s birth, John also took to Twitter to tell the world that he has become a second-time father.

Violet wasn’t up to two years when her dad on Ellen DeGeneres Show revealed that the youngster already has an ear for music.

Hazel Krasinski was Born After Her Parents Got Married

John Krasinski and his wife, Emily Blunt, were already four years into their marriage when Hazel was born.

The celebrity duo met at a restaurant through a mutual friend, but prior to that, The Office alum was already crushing on Emily after he watched her movie, The Devil Wears Prada. He later confessed to his spouse that he watched the film over 70 times and was glad she chose to remain with her stalker. It was love at first sight for John, who has since admitted that the chemistry was instant and undeniable.

The couple had their first date at John’s apartment, where they went for a Pizza. John’s rep said they became officially engaged in August 2009, but the details were kept private. However, the American actor said it was such a happy and emotional moment that reduced them to tears.

A year after their official engagement, Hazel Krasinski’s parents exchanged wedding vows on July 10th, 2010, and the ceremony was hosted in Lake Como, Italy, precisely at George Clooney’s estate. Reports said it was a quiet affair with just family and friends in attendance.

The couple’s union has endured for almost 13 years and still counting, and from what is perceivable, they may take it as far into the future as possible.

Hazel May be Following in her Parent’s Footsteps

John Krasinski was on the set of The Ellen DeGeneres Show in February 2020 and revealed that his first daughter loves to crack jokes. He described both his daughters as big jokesters with big hearts. With how Hazel is going, it would not be surprising if she also chooses to forge a career in the entertainment industry like her mum and dad.

Hazel Krasinki is also turning out to be a philanthropist. The young lady and her baby sister assisted in raising money for the hospitality industry during the peak of the Corona Virus pandemic. The Krasinki girls designed tote bags and t-shirts with all the proceeds paid to Guy Fieri’s initiative with RERF (Restaurant Employee Relief Fund).
