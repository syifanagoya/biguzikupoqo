Bebe Neuwirth Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, March 8, 2024

Bebe Neuwirth, known for her portrayal of Dr. Lilith Sternin on “Cheers” and “Frasier,” as well as Nadine Toliver on “Madam Secretary,” has a net worth of $10 million. She began her career in the early 1980s, and apart from her screen appearances, she has also been successful in stage productions like “Sweet Charity”, “Chicago”, and “The Addams Family”. She has a background in ballet and attended the prestigious Julliard School in New York City. Neuwirth has also appeared in various films, including “Green Card,” “Bugsy,” and “Jumanji.” On television, she has also been part of series such as “Law & Order: Trial by Jury,” “Bored To Death,” “The Good Wife,” and “Blue Bloods.” In addition to her entertainment career, she is an animal welfare advocate. Bebe Neuwirth has received accolades for her work, including Primetime Emmy and Tony Awards. She is currently married to Chris Calkins.
